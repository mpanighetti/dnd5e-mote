# Malagwan Expeditions

**Malagwan Expeditions** is an adventuring guild taking jobs along the west coast of [Lenya](../../ch-1-welcome-to-mote/esterfell/lenya/lenya.md) and across the [Strait of Shallows](../../ch-1-welcome-to-mote/esterfell/waters/strait-of-shallows.md) to [Malagwa](../../ch-1-welcome-to-mote/esterfell/malagwa.md) and [Barria](../../ch-1-welcome-to-mote/esterfell/barria.md). Sellswords affiliated with Malagwan Expeditions operate out of [Fleuderton](../../societies/esterfell-accord/fleuderton/fleuderton.md), using the [Siren's Shanty](../../societies/esterfell-accord/fleuderton/sirens-shanty.md) as a base of operations.
