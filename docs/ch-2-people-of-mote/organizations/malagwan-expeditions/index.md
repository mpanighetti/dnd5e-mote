# Malagwan Expeditions

**Malagwan Expeditions** is an adventuring guild taking jobs along the west coast of [Lenya](../../../ch-4-esterfell-gazetteer/esterfell/lenya/) and across the [Strait of Shallows](../../../ch-4-esterfell-gazetteer/coasts/index.md#strait-of-shallows) to [Malagwa](../../../ch-4-esterfell-gazetteer/esterfell/malagwa.md) and [Barria](../../../ch-4-esterfell-gazetteer/esterfell/barria.md). Sellswords affiliated with Malagwan Expeditions operate out of [Fleuderton](../../societies/esterfell-accord/fleuderton/), using the [Siren's Shanty](../../societies/esterfell-accord/fleuderton/sirens-shanty.md) as a base of operations.
