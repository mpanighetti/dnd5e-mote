**Chanticleer** runs the [Bridgeport](../../../societies/esterfell-accord/bridgeport.md) chapter of [Reynard's Den](../reynards-den.md).

## Information

### Mental

- pronouns: he/him
- languages: Common, Auran, Elvish

### Physical

- species: aarakocra
